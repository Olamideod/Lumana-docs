# Disappearing

Disappearing detection fires when an object exits a zone you've set in the camera view. It's the complement to the [appearing alert](appearing.md): instead of watching for arrivals, it watches for departures.

## How it works

After you define a zone in the camera frame, Lumana monitors it for objects leaving. When an object of the configured type exits the zone, the alert fires and a clip is saved.

## When to use it

This alert works best for zones where objects are expected to stay put, and any departure is worth investigating.

- Detecting when an asset or piece of equipment leaves a designated storage area
- Monitoring zones where objects should stay, such as exhibits, displays, or secure items on a shelf
- Tracking when a vehicle or person departs an area outside of expected times
