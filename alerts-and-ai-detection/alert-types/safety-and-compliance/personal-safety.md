# Personal safety

Personal safety detection fires when a person is too close to a vehicle for too long. Use it in environments where forklifts, heavy equipment, or other vehicles operate near pedestrians.

## How it works

Lumana monitors the positions of detected people and vehicles in the camera frame. When a person remains within an unsafe distance of a vehicle past the configured time threshold, Lumana triggers the alert and saves a clip for review.

## When to use it

Personal safety detection is well suited for any environment where the risk of a pedestrian-vehicle collision is a concern.

- Monitoring warehouse aisles and loading docks where forklifts operate near workers
- Detecting pedestrians who enter vehicle lanes or travel paths in a facility
- Supplementing physical barriers and safety markings with automated visual monitoring
