# Smoking and vaping detection

The smoking and vaping alert triggers when a person is detected smoking or vaping in a monitored area. Use it to enforce no-smoking policies, monitor compliance in designated smoke-free zones, or flag violations in regulated environments.

## How it works

Lumana analyzes the video feed for visual indicators of smoking or vaping behavior. When detected within the configured zone or camera view, the alert triggers and captures a clip of the incident.

## When to use it

This alert is most effective in environments where smoking or vaping poses a safety, compliance, or liability risk.

- Offices, warehouses, or public spaces with no-smoking policies
- Healthcare facilities, schools, or hospitality venues with strict compliance requirements
- Areas with fire or safety hazards where smoking is prohibited, such as fuel storage or clean rooms

> **Note:** For best results, position cameras to capture the upper body of individuals in the monitored area. Poor lighting or obstructions may reduce detection accuracy.

## Configure the alert

1. Select **Alerts** in the left navigation bar, then select **Configurations**.
2. Select **Add alert**.
3. Under the **Safety & compliance** category, select **Smoking and vaping**.
4. Give the alert a name.
5. Select the camera or cameras to monitor.
6. Draw a zone if you want to restrict detection to a specific area of the frame.
7. Set the time frame for when the alert should be active.
8. Select **Then do this** to configure notifications.
9. Select **Create alert**.
