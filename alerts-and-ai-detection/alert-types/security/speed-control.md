# Speed control

Speed control alerts you when something in the camera view is moving too fast. You set a threshold for your environment, and Lumana flags any person or vehicle that exceeds it.

## How it works

Lumana measures the movement speed of detected objects in the camera frame. When an object crosses the configured speed threshold, the alert triggers and a clip is saved. You can tune the threshold to fit your environment, whether that's people running in a controlled space or vehicles exceeding a safe speed on a premises.

## When to use it

Speed control detection is well suited for environments where the pace of movement directly affects safety.

- Monitoring warehouse or industrial environments where fast-moving forklifts or vehicles pose safety risks.
- Enforcing no-running policies in sensitive areas like hospitals, data centers, or labs.
- Detecting rapid movement near secured zones that may indicate a breach or emergency.

In each of these environments, the speed of movement is a direct proxy for risk. [Door state change](doors.md) gives you a similar early-warning signal at access points, using the camera to monitor when doors open, close, or stay open too long.
