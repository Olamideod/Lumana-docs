# Zone armed/disarmed

The zone armed/disarmed alert triggers when a zone's armed status changes. Use it to track when monitoring is enabled or disabled for a specific area, or to catch unexpected changes to your security setup.

## How it works

Each zone in Lumana can be armed or disarmed independently. When the armed status of a zone changes, the alert triggers and logs the event with a timestamp. You can configure it to trigger on arming, disarming, or both.

## When to use it

This alert is useful for auditing zone arming activity and detecting unauthorized changes to your monitoring configuration.

- Verifying that zones are armed at the start of a shift or after hours.
- Detecting when a zone is disarmed unexpectedly during a monitored period.
- Auditing arm/disarm events across multiple locations for compliance reporting.

These scenarios all depend on tracking armed status as part of an audit trail, not just monitoring what's in the camera view.
