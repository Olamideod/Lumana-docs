# Shelf occupancy threshold

Shelf occupancy threshold triggers when inventory on a monitored shelf drops below a level you set. Use it to catch low-stock conditions in real time from a camera feed, without manual shelf checks.

## How it works

Lumana monitors the occupancy level of a defined shelf area in the camera view. When detected inventory falls below your configured threshold, the alert triggers and captures a clip. You set the threshold based on the minimum acceptable stock level for that shelf.

## When to use it

This alert is a good fit for retail or warehouse environments where stock levels directly affect operations or customer experience.

- Getting notified when a high-demand shelf is running low so staff can restock before it empties.
- Monitoring critical inventory locations where out-of-stock conditions cause the most disruption.
- Reducing the need for manual shelf walks by automating low-stock detection across multiple cameras.

These scenarios share a common goal: acting on a stock level before the consequence reaches customers or operations.
