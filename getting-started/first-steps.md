# First steps

Follow these steps in order to get Lumana up and running for the first time.

## 1. Set up Lumana Core

Lumana Core is the hardware that connects your cameras to the platform. Choose the installation guide that matches your network environment:

* [Standard network installation](set-up-lumana-core/standard-network-installation.md)
* [Isolated network installation](set-up-lumana-core/isolated-network-installation.md)
* [Dual-network installation](set-up-lumana-core/dual-network-installation.md)

<!-- IMAGE: lumana-core-device.png — A photo or product shot of the Lumana Core hardware device, showing its physical ports (Ethernet, power). Clean background. -->

## 2. Connect your cameras

Once Lumana Core is online, add your cameras to the system. The steps vary slightly by camera brand. See [Connect a camera](connect-a-camera.md) for instructions.

<!-- IMAGE: connect-camera-web-app.png — A screenshot of the Lumana web app showing the camera connection or discovery screen, where users add a new camera. -->

## 3. Install the mobile app

Download the Lumana mobile app to monitor your cameras and manage alerts from your phone. See [Use the Lumana mobile app](use-the-lumana-mobile-app.md) for installation and setup.

## 4. Configure your first alert

With cameras connected, set up an alert to start detecting activity. See [Configure alerts](../alerts-and-ai-detection/configure-alerts.md) to create your first alert rule.

With your Core online, cameras connected, and your first alert active, Lumana is ready to monitor your environment.
